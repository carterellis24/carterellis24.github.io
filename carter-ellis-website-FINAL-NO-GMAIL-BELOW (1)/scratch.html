Why the University of Michigan is the Best College Football Program

The Michigan Wolverines are more than just a team—they’re the winningest, most iconic college football program in NCAA history.

Highlight Video

Top Verified Reasons

  • Most wins in NCAA Division I FBS history – over 1,000 victories (1st all-time)
  • Claimed 12 national championships – including 2023
  • 44 Big Ten Conference Championships – most in conference history
  • The Big House – Michigan Stadium is the largest stadium in the U.S. (capacity 107,601+)
  • Legendary rivalry with Ohio State – "The Game" is one of the most storied in sports
  • Produced 3 Heisman Trophy winners – Tom Harmon, Desmond Howard, Charles Woodson
  • Over 400 NFL draftees – including Tom Brady, Charles Woodson, Aidan Hutchinson
  • Iconic coaches – Fielding Yost, Bo Schembechler, Lloyd Carr, Jim Harbaugh
  • Massive, passionate fanbase – consistently leads the nation in average attendance
  • Elite academics + athletics – regularly ranked top public university with top-tier sports
